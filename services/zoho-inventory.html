Common Questions

Zoho Inventory FAQs

Everything you need to know before starting your Zoho Inventory implementation.

What is Zoho Inventory and how does it differ from Zoho Books?

+
Zoho Books is accounting software that handles invoicing, expenses, and financial reporting. Zoho Inventory is specifically built for managing stock, purchase orders, sales orders, warehouses, and fulfilment. The two integrate seamlessly — Zoho Inventory handles the operational side (what you have, where it is, what's ordered) while Zoho Books handles the financial side (what it cost, what you earned).

Can Zoho Inventory handle multiple warehouses?

+
Yes, fully. You can set up any number of warehouses or storage locations in Zoho Inventory. Each warehouse has its own stock levels, and you can transfer items between warehouses, set warehouse-specific reorder points, and fulfil orders from the most appropriate location based on rules I configure for you.

Does Zoho Inventory sync with Shopify in real time?

+
Yes. The Zoho Inventory–Shopify integration I set up provides real-time, two-way sync. When an order is placed on Shopify, it appears immediately in Zoho Inventory. When stock is updated in Zoho Inventory (after a purchase or adjustment), the levels push back to your Shopify store automatically. This eliminates overselling and manual stock updates entirely.

Can I track batch numbers and expiry dates?

+
Yes. Zoho Inventory supports batch tracking and serial number tracking. You can assign batch numbers at the time of purchase receipt, track expiry dates, and configure FEFO (first-expiry-first-out) fulfilment rules. This is especially important for food, pharma, and chemical businesses where regulatory compliance requires lot traceability.

How does the Zoho Books integration work?

+
When you receive goods against a purchase order in Zoho Inventory, a corresponding bill is created in Zoho Books automatically. When you fulfil a sales order, an invoice is generated in Zoho Books. The cost of goods sold (COGS) updates in real time based on your valuation method (FIFO or average cost). This means your accounts always reflect your actual inventory position without any manual data entry.

Can Zoho Inventory replace Tally for inventory management?

+
For most growing businesses, yes — Zoho Inventory combined with Zoho Books provides a modern, cloud-based alternative to Tally for inventory and accounting needs. It's accessible from anywhere, integrates with e-commerce and logistics platforms, and provides real-time visibility that Tally cannot match. I handle the complete migration of your item master, opening stock, and transaction history from Tally to Zoho.

What happens to my existing item data during migration?

+
I handle your complete item master migration — including SKUs, descriptions, units, tax rates, vendor details, and opening stock quantities. Data is cleaned, deduplicated, and validated before import. We run a parallel period to verify stock counts match before cutting over fully. Zero data loss is standard in every implementation I deliver.

Is Zoho Inventory available on mobile?

+
Yes. Zoho Inventory has a full-featured mobile app for Android and iOS. Warehouse staff can scan barcodes to receive stock, confirm dispatches, and check item availability from their phones. This is particularly useful for warehouses and field sales teams who need live stock visibility without being at a desktop.
