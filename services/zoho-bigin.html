Common Questions

Zoho Bigin FAQs

Everything you need to know about Zoho Bigin before getting started.

What is Zoho Bigin and who is it designed for?

+
Zoho Bigin is a simple, affordable pipeline-based CRM built specifically for small businesses, freelancers, and startups. It focuses on what small teams actually need: contact management, deal tracking, email sync, and follow-up reminders — without the overwhelming features and setup complexity of a full enterprise CRM. It's ideal for teams of 1 to 15 people.

How is Zoho Bigin different from Zoho CRM?

+
Zoho Bigin is simpler, faster to set up, and more affordable — designed for small businesses who need core CRM features. Zoho CRM is a full enterprise platform with advanced customisation, AI (Zia), Blueprint, territory management, and complex automation. I recommend Bigin when your team is under 15 people and you need a CRM running fast. When you scale, I migrate everything to Zoho CRM with zero data loss.

How quickly can Zoho Bigin be set up?

+
The Bigin Starter package goes live in 24–48 hours. The Professional package — with WhatsApp integration, web form connection, automations, and team setup — typically takes 3 to 5 business days. This includes your contact import, pipeline configuration, and training. No other CRM implementation I know of gets your team productive this fast.

Does Zoho Bigin work with WhatsApp?

+
Yes. I connect Zoho Bigin with WhatsApp Business through Zoho Flow and third-party connectors. This means messages from WhatsApp can be logged against contacts in Bigin, and you can manage all your customer conversations in one place rather than juggling between a CRM and a chat app. This is one of the most popular features I configure for small business teams in India.

Can I use Zoho Bigin on my phone?

+
Yes. Zoho Bigin has a highly rated mobile app for both Android and iOS. Your team can log calls, update deal stages, add notes, and create follow-up tasks directly from their phones. This is particularly valuable for field sales teams, real estate agents, and service businesses whose team members are always on the move.

What happens to my data if I upgrade to Zoho CRM later?

+
Zoho has specifically designed Bigin as the entry point to Zoho CRM. When you're ready to upgrade, I handle the full migration — contacts, deals, activity history, notes, and custom fields — all transferred to Zoho CRM with zero data loss. Your team can continue working without any interruption. This is included as a free consultation in the Bigin Plus package.

Can I import my existing contacts from Excel or Google Sheets?

+
Yes — this is a standard part of every package I offer. I clean, format, and import your existing contacts, leads, and company data from Excel, Google Sheets, or any previous CRM into Zoho Bigin. Records are deduplicated and mapped correctly to the right fields before import, so your data is clean and ready to use from day one.

How much does Zoho Bigin cost per month?

+
Zoho Bigin has a free plan for up to 3 users with basic features. The paid Express plan starts at approximately ₹599 per user per month (billed annually), and the Premier plan is around ₹999 per user per month. These are Zoho's subscription fees — separate from my one-time implementation fee. I help you choose the right plan and ensure you're not paying for features you don't need.
