Common Questions

Zoho People FAQs

Everything you need to know about Zoho People before getting started.

What is Zoho People and what does it automate?

+
Zoho People is a cloud-based HR management system that automates all core HR operations — employee onboarding, leave management, attendance tracking, shift scheduling, performance appraisals, and payroll preparation. It includes a mobile app and employee self-service portal, making all HR processes paperless, accurate, and fully automated — freeing your HR team from repetitive admin work.

Can Zoho People track attendance with geo-fencing?

+
Yes. Zoho People supports geo-fencing attendance, meaning employees can only mark attendance when physically present at a defined location — office, construction site, factory floor, or branch. It also supports QR code check-in, biometric device integration, and facial recognition. This is particularly useful for field teams, multi-site construction companies, and businesses with multiple branches.

Does Zoho People integrate with Zoho Payroll?

+
Yes — Zoho People and Zoho Payroll are designed to work together. Leave balances, attendance data, overtime hours, and LOP (Loss of Pay) from Zoho People sync automatically to Zoho Payroll, eliminating manual payroll calculation errors. I set up the full integration so your payroll team has accurate, real-time HR data every month — payroll that used to take 3 days takes 3 hours.

Can employees manage their own leave and payslips?

+
Yes. Zoho People includes a comprehensive employee self-service portal where employees can apply for leave, check their attendance records, view and download payslips, submit expense claims, update personal information, and access HR documents — without involving HR manually. Most HR teams save 10–15 hours of administrative work per week from self-service alone.

How long does Zoho People implementation take?

+
The Essentials package goes live in 5–7 business days. The Professional package — with geo-fencing, payroll integration, performance management, and onboarding workflows — typically takes 2–3 weeks. Enterprise implementations with biometric devices and complex multi-branch configurations take 4–6 weeks. All timelines include data migration, configuration, and training.

Can Zoho People handle multiple branches or locations?

+
Yes. Zoho People is built for multi-location businesses. You can configure separate leave policies, holiday calendars, attendance rules, shift schedules, and geo-fencing zones for each branch or department. All data rolls up into a single HR dashboard, giving management complete visibility across all locations with branch-level drill-down.

Does Zoho People work for performance appraisals?

+
Yes. Zoho People has a comprehensive performance management module that supports goal setting, KPI tracking, continuous feedback, mid-year reviews, 360-degree feedback, and annual appraisal cycles. I configure the entire system to match your existing evaluation framework — rating scales, weightages, review periods, and the approval workflow for finalising appraisals.

How much does Zoho People cost per employee per month?

+
Zoho People has multiple subscription plans starting from approximately ₹48 per employee per month (Essential) up to ₹120+ per employee per month (Enterprise) billed annually. These are Zoho's subscription fees, separate from my one-time implementation cost. I help you choose the right plan for your headcount and feature requirements — ensuring you're not paying for features you won't use.
