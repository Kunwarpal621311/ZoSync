Common Questions

Zoho Creator FAQs

Everything you need to know before getting started with your Zoho Creator app.

What is Zoho Creator and who is it for?

+
Zoho Creator is a low-code application development platform that lets businesses build custom web and mobile applications without heavy software development. It's ideal for any business that relies on Excel, paper forms, or manual processes to run day-to-day operations. Industries from manufacturing and construction to healthcare and education use it to automate their unique workflows.

How long does it take to build a Zoho Creator app?

+
Simple single-module apps (replacing one form or spreadsheet) typically go live in 3–5 days. Multi-module apps with approval workflows and integrations take 1–3 weeks. Complex enterprise applications with external system integrations and custom portals can take 4–8 weeks. I'll give you a specific timeline during the free consultation after understanding your requirements.

Does Zoho Creator work on mobile phones?

+
Yes. Every Zoho Creator app I build is fully mobile-responsive and works on Android and iOS devices through the Zoho Creator mobile app or a browser. Field teams can use it offline — data syncs automatically when they're back online. This makes it ideal for field inspections, delivery confirmation, sales order capture, and any on-the-go use case.

Can Zoho Creator integrate with Zoho CRM and Zoho Books?

+
Yes — Zoho Creator integrates natively with all Zoho products including Zoho CRM, Zoho Books, Zoho People, Zoho Desk, and more. This means data flows automatically between your Creator app and the rest of your Zoho ecosystem. I also connect Creator apps to external systems like Tally, SAP, payment gateways, and WhatsApp via REST APIs and Zoho Flow.

Do I need to know coding to use the app once it's built?

+
No. Once your Zoho Creator app is built and deployed, your team uses it like any normal web or mobile application — clicking buttons, filling forms, and viewing reports. No coding knowledge is required. I design every app with non-technical users in mind, with clear layouts, helpful tooltips, and simple navigation.

What is Deluge scripting and do I need it?

+
Deluge is Zoho's proprietary scripting language used to add custom business logic inside Zoho Creator. For example: automatically calculating a value, sending a conditional notification, validating data against another module, or calling an external API. Most real-world Creator apps require some Deluge scripting. I handle all Deluge code — you just tell me what the app should do.

Can Zoho Creator replace my existing legacy software?

+
In many cases, yes. Zoho Creator is specifically well-suited to replacing older on-premise software, Microsoft Access databases, and complex spreadsheet systems. I've helped businesses migrate from 10-year-old legacy tools to Creator apps that are faster, mobile-accessible, and cloud-hosted. I also handle historical data migration so nothing is lost in the transition.

What happens if I need changes after the app goes live?

+
All packages include a post-launch support period (30 or 60 days). For ongoing changes and enhancements, I offer monthly AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) retainer packages. These cover new feature additions, form modifications, report changes, user management, and any bug fixes — so your app evolves as your business does.
