Payroll for Real Estate Agents

Commission, Incentives &
Payroll — All Automated.

Real estate payroll is complex — fixed salary + variable commissions + incentives per deal closed. Zoho People + Zoho Payroll handles all of it automatically, connected to your CRM deal data.

💰
Auto-Commission Calculation
When a deal is marked Closed in Zoho CRM, the commission is automatically calculated and added to the agent's payroll for that month. Zero manual entry.
📅
Leave & Attendance Management
Zoho People tracks agent attendance, leave balances, and field visits. LOP deductions sync automatically to payroll — no HR chasing agents for attendance sheets.
📊
Incentive Slabs & Targets
Configure incentive slabs based on deals closed per month. 3 closures = 5% bonus. 5+ closures = 10% bonus. Zoho Payroll calculates and pays automatically.
🧾
Payslips & TDS Compliance
Digital payslips auto-generated and shared with every agent via self-service portal. TDS deductions, Form 16, and PF/ESI compliance handled automatically.

Zoho Forms KYC

Digital KYC That Cuts
Booking Time from 9 Days
to 9 Hours.

Replace paper forms and email document chains with a branded Zoho Form that collects all KYC details, Aadhaar, PAN, and property documents digitally — linked directly to the buyer's CRM record.

📋
Custom KYC Form for Each Project
Separate branded Zoho Forms for each property project — residential, commercial, plotted. Collects all buyer details, co-applicant info, and financial documents.
📂
Document Upload in the Form
Buyers upload Aadhaar, PAN, bank statements, ITR, and passport photos directly in the form. Files auto-attached to the CRM lead record.
🔄
Auto-Synced to CRM & WhatsApp
When KYC is submitted, the CRM deal moves to "KYC Received" stage automatically. A WhatsApp acknowledgment is sent to the buyer instantly.
✅
Sales Manager Approval Workflow
KYC submitted → sales manager receives approval task → one-click approve or request additional documents. Entire process tracked with timestamps.
